About the Legal Division

Our Mandate

The Legal Division is the primary arm of the Department of Agrarian Reform responsible for providing legal support, handling agrarian law implementation (ALI) cases, and ensuring that the rights of both landowners and agrarian reform beneficiaries are protected under Philippine law.

Legal Counseling

Providing expert legal advice on land tenure improvement and agrarian justice delivery.

Representation

Representing the department in judicial and quasi-judicial bodies for land-related litigation.
