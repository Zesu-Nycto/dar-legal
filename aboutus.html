Our History

A legacy of agrarian justice

1988

Establishment of DAR

The Department of Agrarian Reform was established under Republic Act No. 6657 to carry out land redistribution.

Early 1990s

Legal Division Formed

Created to handle the volume of agrarian disputes and land conversion cases in the region.

Present

Continued Service

Adjudicating cases and ensuring that the rights of rural workers are protected in Albay Province.

Mandate

Providing legal assistance and adjudication services in the implementation of agrarian reform laws.
