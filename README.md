# DAR Legal Division — Case Management Portal

> Official web portal of the **Department of Agrarian Reform Legal Division**, Province of Albay, Philippines.

---

## 📌 About

This portal serves as the public-facing website of the DAR Legal Division under the Provincial Office of Albay. It provides citizens, farmers, and agrarian reform beneficiaries with access to legal information, case updates, transparency reports, and contact details.

## 📜 Legal Basis

This portal complies with:
- **Republic Act No. 6657** — Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Law (CARL)
- **Executive Order No. 2, s. 2016** — Freedom of Information (FOI)
- **Section 93, General Appropriations Act** — Transparency Seal
