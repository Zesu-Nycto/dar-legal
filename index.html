Legal Division for Agrarian Justice

Ensuring that land distribution and farmer protection are carried out with absolute adherence to the rule of law.

Vision

A just and equitable agrarian society where farmers and rural workers enjoy security of tenure, access to land, and the full protection of the law.

Mission

To provide timely, impartial, and accessible legal services that uphold agrarian rights, resolve land disputes, and implement agrarian reform policies with integrity and excellence.
