Legal Division for Agrarian Justice

Ensuring that land distribution and farmer protection are carried out with absolute adherence to the rule of law.

Vision

A just and equitable agrarian society where farmers and rural workers enjoy security of tenure, access to land, and the full protection of the law.

Mission

To provide timely, impartial, and accessible legal services that uphold agrarian rights, resolve land disputes, and implement agrarian reform policies with integrity and excellence.

Our Services

What We Do

The DAR Legal Division provides comprehensive legal services to protect the rights of farmers and agrarian reform beneficiaries across Albay.

Case Adjudication

Timely and impartial resolution of Agrarian Law Implementation (ALI) cases through rigorous legal review and due process.

Mediation & ADR

Promoting Alternative Dispute Resolution to settle agrarian conflicts fairly and efficiently outside of formal court proceedings.

Legal Assistance

Providing free legal guidance and assistance to agrarian reform beneficiaries and farmers across the Province of Albay.
